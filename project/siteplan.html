Capital City Sewing Club - Site Plan

Site Name:

This name represents a sewing club that is based in Abu Dhabi, the capital city of the UAE.

Site Purpose:

This site provides a resource for memberes of the sewing club, a place where they can get information about the club, the projects that are available, and other relevant sewing information.

Scenarios:

  • What projects are members currently working on?

  • Where and when does the club meet?

  • What size quilt should I make for a queen size bed?
